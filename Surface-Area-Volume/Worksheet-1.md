
# CLASS 9 – WORKSHEET

## Surface Area & Volume

## Section A – Standard Problems

### Q1.

The **dome of a building** is in the shape of a **hemisphere** with radius **7 m**.

Find the **cost of painting the dome** at the rate of **₹5 per m²**.

---

### Q2.

A **conical tent** has:

* Radius = **3 m**
* Height = **4 m**

Find the **area of canvas required** to make the tent.

---

### Q3.

A **hemispherical bowl** has radius **14 cm**.

Find its **curved surface area**.

---

### Q4.

A **solid cone** of:

* Radius = **7 cm**
* Height = **24 cm**

is melted to form a **sphere**.

Find the **radius of the sphere**.

---

### Q5.

A **solid sphere** of radius **7 cm** is melted and recast into a **cone** of the same radius.

Find the **height of the cone**.

---

### Q6.

A **sphere** and a **hemisphere** have the **same radius**.

Find:

1. The **ratio of their surface areas**
2. The **ratio of their volumes**

---
